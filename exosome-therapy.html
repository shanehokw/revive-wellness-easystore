Questions about Exosome Therapy

What is exosome therapy?

Exosome therapy is a cutting-edge treatment that utilizes nano-sized particles derived from mesenchymal stem cells to stimulate healing and rejuvenation in the body.

How do exosomes work in therapy?

Exosomes act as messengers, facilitating cell-to-cell communication to initiate the healing and regenerative processes in the body.

Where do the exosomes used in therapy come from?

Our exosomes are extracted from donated umbilical cord tissues and then purified and characterized to ensure the highest quality and purity.

What are the benefits of exosome therapy?

Exosome therapy has undergone rigorous clinical trials and has been shown to have anti-inflammatory properties, promote collagen production, stimulate the growth of new cells, and improve skin texture, among other benefits.
